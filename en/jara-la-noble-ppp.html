A story with heart

A children's story about empathy, education and the importance of getting to know someone before judging them. Jara is a PPP, but above all she is loving, loyal, protective, intelligent and noble.

A story for children and adults that reminds us that appearances never tell the whole story.

More than a story

Jara also has a mission 🐾

Jara was born from a very simple idea: no dog should be judged solely by its breed or by a label.

That is why Juan Manuel wants to share copies of this story with animal shelters and rescue centres in his area, so Jara can reach children and families and help them see every dog for what it truly is: an individual with its own story and its own heart.
