What is the origin of the Tooth Fairy Mouse?

If you are wondering what the origin of the Tooth Fairy Mouse is or where the Tooth Fairy Mouse was born, this page invites you to discover a children's story inspired by tradition and Jerez de la Frontera.

The Origin of the Tooth Fairy Mouse is a story by Juan Manuel Vela Vacas that imagines Pérez's roots among wineries, wine casks and the streets of Jerez. A story for children and adults that turns a familiar tradition into an adventure full of magic.

Discover the story that inspired the tradition of the Tooth Fairy Mouse and how this character was born in Jerez de la Frontera. A Spanish story with more than 100 years of history that has travelled around the world, now told in a children's book by Juan Manuel Vela Vacas.

If you are searching for the origin of the Tooth Fairy Mouse, this story invites you to discover a magical and literary version of its roots.
