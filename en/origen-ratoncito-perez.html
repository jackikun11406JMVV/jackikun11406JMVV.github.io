A tradition passed down from generation to generation.

The Tooth Fairy Mouse is part of children's imagination and a tradition many families share with their children when a tooth falls out.

The literary tradition of the character is linked to Luis Coloma, a figure who is part of the history of the Tooth Fairy Mouse and the way the character has reached us.

But when a child asks “Where does the Tooth Fairy Mouse come from?”, they are often not looking only for a date or a name. They are looking for a story. They want to know how Pérez can visit so many homes, how he knows all the children and why each family experiences the magic in its own way.

The story behind the book

And then,
Juanito Pérez was born in Jerez.

This is the literary and magical version Juan Manuel Vela Vacas imagined for his book.

In The Origin of the Tooth Fairy Mouse, the story begins in Jerez de la Frontera. Among wineries, barrels and the atmosphere of a city full of tradition, we meet a little mouse who is about to become someone very special.

It is a story created so children can imagine an answer to all those questions that appear when tooth night arrives.

Questions children ask

What does this book bring to children's reading?

With this book, children can find answers to many of the questions they may have when the Tooth Fairy Mouse comes to their home. Through imagination and magic, the story accompanies those questions and helps make sense of them.

The story explores questions such as:

How can the Tooth Fairy Mouse reach every home in the world? Does the Tooth Fairy and the Tooth Fairy Mouse really exist? How can he be in several places at the same time? Where does he come from? What is his story?

And also those small differences that children notice so much: why does my Tooth Fairy Mouse leave sweets while my friend gets money? Why do I leave my tooth in a glass while my friend leaves it under the pillow?

Many of these answers are within the pages of The Origin of the Tooth Fairy Mouse.

Questions about the author

Who is Juan Manuel Vela Vacas?

Juan Manuel Vela Vacas is the author of The Origin of the Tooth Fairy Mouse and Jara, the Noble PPP. Learn more about the author →

Where was the Tooth Fairy Mouse born?

In Juan Manuel Vela Vacas' literary version, the Tooth Fairy Mouse was born in Jerez de la Frontera.

How did you come up with the idea for this book?

After hearing that Father Luis Coloma, author of the first literary description of the Tooth Fairy Mouse, was born in Jerez, Juan Manuel began to imagine… what if, when he was young, Coloma had met some friendly winery mice there, including the famous Pérez? That question gave birth to the legend that inspires this story.
