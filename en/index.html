About the author

Hello, I'm Juan Manuel.

I'm from Jerez and I love turning ideas, memories and little stories into tales that children and adults can enjoy together.

I'm inspired by tradition, animals and that special magic that appears when a story manages to stay in your memory.

“What if the Tooth Fairy Mouse had been born in Jerez?”

From an idea to a story 📖

When I discovered that Father Luis Coloma, the author of the first literary description of the Ratoncito Pérez, was born in Jerez, I started to imagine...

What if it had all begun here?

That is how El origen del Ratoncito Pérez was born: a story of wine cellars, little mice and tradition.
